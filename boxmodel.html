Shikhar Dhawan

Shikhar Dhawan is a flamboyant left-handed opener known for aggressive starts. He shines in ICC tournaments with consistent runs. His cheerful nature and experience make him a team favorite.

Sachin Tendulkar

Sachin Tendulkar is the legendary “God of Cricket” and one of the greatest batsmen ever. His records, discipline, and humility inspire millions. Even after retirement, he remains a cricketing icon.

Shreyas Iyer

Shreyas Iyer is a stylish middle-order batsman who anchors innings under pressure. He has also shown leadership in the IPL. His stroke play against both pace and spin makes him dependable.

Ravindra Jadeja

Ravindra Jadeja is a true all-rounder with sharp left-arm spin, useful batting, and electric fielding. He often turns games with his all-round skills. His energy adds balance and strength to the team.

Jasprit Bumrah

Jasprit Bumrah is India’s top fast bowler, famous for his yorkers and unorthodox action. He shines in death overs and handles pressure calmly. His consistency makes him a match-winner in all formats.
